How is payment made in the app?

Payment is made completely between the buyer and seller. BeautyPop is not involved in the payment between users, thus here are a few suggested ways where deals could be completed:

  1. Bank transfer and mailing

  2. Cash on delivery and meet-ups

  3. Paypal

For more information on postage rates, you may visit: http://www.hongkongpost.hk/en/postage_calculator
