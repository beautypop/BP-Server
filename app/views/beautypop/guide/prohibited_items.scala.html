What items are prohibited on BeautyPop?

BeautyPop prohibits the listing or sale of any items that are illegal under any applicable law, statute, ordinance or regulation at a local, state, provincial, national or international level.

Some examples of items that are not allowed:

  1. Photos that do not show the item clearly.
  2. Internet photos, i.e. you have not taken the picture yourself.
  3. Item infringes trademarks, copyrights or any other IP rights of third parties.
  4. Item is perishable (food etc.) or live animals.
  5. Advertisements.
  6. Photo or text containing a reference to another website, app or merchant.
  7. Items that are defective or of poor quality.
  8. An item listed as "free" but is not intended to be given away.
  9. There is no realistic price set for the item.
  10. Duplicated listings.

Other items that are specifically prohibited include:

  1. Alcoholic Beverages
  2. Blood, Bodily Fluids and Body Parts
  3. Burglary Tools
  4. Controlled chemical substances (such as cadmium, mercury, acids)
  5. Counterfeit currency, stamps or coins
  6. Counterfeit Products
  7. Databases containing personal data
  8. Electronic Surveillance Equipment
  9. Embargoed Goods
  10. Government, official and Transit Uniforms, IDs and Licenses
  11. Illegal Drugs and Drug Paraphernalia
  12. All types of illegal services and products
  13. Hacking tools
  14. Hazardous Materials
  15. Fireworks, Destructive Devices and Explosives
  16. Identity Documents, Personal Financial Records and Personal Information (in any form, including mailing lists).
  17. Items received through Government assistance
  18. Items which encourage or facilitate illegal activity
  19. Lottery Tickets, Sweepstakes Entries and Slot Machines
  20. Obscene Material and all types of Pornography
  21. All type of offensive material
  22. Pesticides
  23. Pictures or images that contain frontal nudity
  24. Police and Other Security Forces Badges and Uniforms
  25. Prescription Drugs, medicines, supplements and Devices
  26. Recalled items
  27. Stocks and Other Securities
  28. Stolen Property
  29. Tobacco Products (including vapes and e cigarettes)
  30. Used Cosmetics
  31. Weapons and related items (such as firearms, firearm parts and magazines, ammunition, BB and pellet guns, tear gas, stun guns, switchblade knives, and martial arts weapons).

In addition, BeautyPop reserves the right to remove any listings that we consider inappropriate or unfit for posting. Our policies are often based on applicable laws and regulations, although in some cases, we may make listing decisions based on input from our users and our own discretion, especially for dangerous or sensitive items.
