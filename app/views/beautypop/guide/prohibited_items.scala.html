What items are prohibited on BeautyPop?

There are certain items that are not allowed on BeautyPop at all. They may not necessarily be illegal, and they may even fit our selling criteria. However, they aren’t in the spirit of BeautyPop. As such, if the items listed below are found on the BeautyPop marketplace, they will be promptly deleted.

  1. Food
  2. Alcohol
  3. Health supplements
  4. Contact Lenses and other optometry products
  5. Medicine, medicinal instruments and items with objectionable medical claims
  6. E-cigarettes and related products
  7. Hazardous materials which are flammable, explosive, corrosive, poisonous or otherwise dangerous
  8. Recalled items
  9. Weapons, including firearms - imitation firearms
  10. Tobacco products and other smokeable products
  11. Drugs and drug paraphenalia 
  12. Live animals and insects
  13. Items made from animals, including, but not limited to, endangered species and exotic animals
  14. Counterfeit items
  15. High powered laser pointers (Class 3b and Class 4)
  16. Items for rent and listings advertising rental services
  17. Items or listings that promote, support or glorify hatred and seek to discriminate and/or demean on the basis of race, religion, gender, gender identity, disability or sexual orientation
  18. Items or listings that promote, support or seek to engage users in illegal activity
  19. Items or listings that promote, support or glorify acts of violence
  20. Items or listings that seek to shame fellow BeautyPop members and/or reveal personal information of fellow BeautyPop members
  21. Items that infringe copyright laws including, but not limited to, unauthorized sales of eBooks
  22. Virtual items including, but not limited to, game currency, game items and game accounts/characters
  23. Unauthorised resale of tickets
  24. Used undergarments and intimate items
  25. Stolen or Illegal goods
  26. Pornography and adult material including, but not limited to, pornographic books, magazines, videos and pictures
  27. Human beings, human remains and body parts
  28. Services
  29. Re-sale of mobile numbers
  30. Multi-Level-Marketing and related products
