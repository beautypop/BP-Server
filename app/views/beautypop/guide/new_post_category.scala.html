Your item can be classified in more than one category. Can you post your item in different categories?

Do only list the item in one category that you feel is most suitable. For example, a moisturising cream can be classified in both 'For Her' and 'Beauty Products', but classifying it into 'Beauty Products' will be the most appropriate choice.

Repeated postings in multiple categories will be taken down by our team without further notice.

If you are unsure of which category to upload your item to, do write in to us at support@baby-box.com.hk and we'll be happy to provide you some advice!
