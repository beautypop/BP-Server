BeautyPop - Photo Guide

A quick guide on how to sell your items faster. Just follow these simple steps.

Picture 1 - Item

This photo should be of the garment laid out on a white, or light, colored surface, lit up by daylight. Make sure to get the entire garment in the image. Keep this photo uncluttred.

Picture 2 - Fit

Use these pictures to enhance cool details of your garment. If your garment is damaged in any way it's a good idea to show it here. If possible, take a picture of yourself wearing the garment. This allows potential customers to quickly get an idea of how it fits and looks in real life.

Picture 3 - Details

Use these pictures to enhance cool details of your garment. If your garment is damaged in any way it's a good idea to show it here. If possible, take a picture of yourself wearing the garment. This allows potential customers to quickly get an idea of how it fits and looks in real life.

Do!

Do center your garment

Do arrange your picture nicely

Do display awesome details

Do fold your garment, to present it well

Don't!

Don't take the picture from a weird angle

Don't add text to your image

Don't use pictures from online, show your version of the garment!

Don't do collages, not cool

Don't use low resolution images

Don't display more than one item per ad

Don't have frames or weird borders around your image
